## Deviations from Plan

**[Rule 1 — missing critical] Pinned versions incompatible with Payload 3.84 peer deps**
Found during: Task 1.01.1 `pnpm install`
Issue: Plan pinned `next@15.3.5`, `react@19.0.0`, `react-dom@19.0.0`. Payload 3.84.1's `@payloadcms/next` declares peer `next: ">=15.2.9 <15.3.0 || >=15.3.9 <15.4.0 ..."` and `@payloadcms/ui` declares `react: "^19.0.1 || ..."`. The plan's pins fall in forbidden gaps.
Fix: Bumped to `next@15.3.9`, `react@19.0.1`, `react-dom@19.0.1`, `eslint-config-next@15.3.9`. All within the plan's stated "Next 15.3.x" line and react 19 minor.
Files modified: `package.json`, `pnpm-lock.yaml`
Verification: `pnpm install` reports 0 unmet peers; `pnpm typecheck` exits 0
Commit hash: `95b68b4`

**[Rule 1 — workaround] Manual scaffold instead of `create-payload-app`**
Found during: Task 1.01.1 Step 1

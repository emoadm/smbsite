Stand up everything email-deliverability needs to begin Phase 1's 4-week warm-up: produce the domain decision artifact (Open Decision #5 / RESEARCH Q2), document the DNS records to add (SPF/DKIM/DMARC per RESEARCH § SPF / DKIM / DMARC Setup), register the domain in Google Postmaster Tools v2, define the warm-up cadence per D-18, and consolidate the Phase 1 sign-off checklist (5 ROADMAP success criteria + manual evidence).

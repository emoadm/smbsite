# Phase 02.2 Plan 01 — Coalition Agenda Content (Walking-Skeleton Slice)

## Outcome

`/agenda` ships the coalition's manifesto, the 'Десен консенсус' top chapter, and the 'Икономика' detailed sub-chapter as hardcoded Bulgarian JSX. A populated 3-entry Table of Contents (Манифест / Десен консенсус / Икономика) renders as a sticky sidebar on md+ and a collapsible `<details>` block on mobile. The draft-mode `<Alert>` banner remains visible — its removal is reserved for Phase 02.3 (final slice).

The architectural contract for Phase 02.3 is now established: hardcoded JSX in `page.tsx`, file-level `lint-i18n` exemption, anchor-drift unit test guarding TOC integrity. Phase 02.3 will extend this same shape for the remaining 9–10 chapters.

## Source Content Ambiguities Encountered

1. **Земеделие bullet count (19 vs ~17):** The planner estimated ~17 bullets. Authoring against the source counted 19 discrete policy items including `Засилване на административния и професионален капацитет на служителите на БАКХ` and `Проекти за допълнителна преработка и усвояване на храни с изтекла годност`. All rendered in the single flat `<ul>` per UI-SPEC §3.3.B Pattern #3.

2. **Здравеопазване sub-section "1." prefix disambiguation:** The source uses "1." in front of each sub-section label (pdftotext per-page numbering reset). Per RESEARCH assumption A4 and planner's discretion call, all are rendered as `<h3>` elements (not `<ol>` items). The verbatim sub-section label for the 4th sub-section is a long compound sentence that was rendered as-is without truncation per §3.3.F.

3. **Външна сигурност nested `<ul>` identification:** The nested sub-items appear under the "Незабавно и ускорено превъоръжаване" item (last substantive bullet, source lines 1093-1101) — matching the plan's description of "one `<li>` item with a nested `<ul>` of ~3 sub-items." The actual count is 2 sub-items (`Спешно освобождаване` and `Преосмисляне на сценариите`), not 3. This deviation from the planner's estimate is a source-fidelity call — both sub-items are present verbatim.

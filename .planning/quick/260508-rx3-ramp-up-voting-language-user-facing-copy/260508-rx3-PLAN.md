# Quick Task 260508-rx3 — Ramp-up без гласуване

## Goal

GDPR Art. 9 legal opinion is still pending; voting (Phase 3) cannot ship until counsel returns. But the platform is functionally ready for real-user ramp-up on Phases 1 / 2 / 2.1 / 02.2 / 5 (registration, agenda, attribution, newsletter). Two surfaces still leak voting language and would mislead first users:

1. **Registration form** — political-opinion consent checkbox referring to "записване на гласовете и авторските ми предложения" (worded for an Art. 9 trigger we don't actually fire yet).
2. **User-facing copy** — landing notify card, feature grid card #3, FAQ proposals answer, welcome email body — all promise "гласуване" / "внасяне на инициативи" features that aren't shipping in this ramp-up.

Reword to match what the platform actually does today (read coalition-authored agenda, receive newsletter), with explicit "когато правното становище разреши, ще активираме" framing for voting.

## Out of Scope

- DB schema (`consents.kind` enum keeps `political_opinion` for re-activation later).
- Phase 3 code (doesn't exist yet).
- Privacy notice page body (only a title now; substantive privacy notice is a separate phase).
- Newsletter topic enums for `newsletter_voting` (valid future newsletter category, not user-facing claim about active features).
- `destructive.deleteAccountConfirm` (describes what *would* be deleted; harmless).
- `.planning/legal/*` (briefs to external counsel must reflect full design intent).
